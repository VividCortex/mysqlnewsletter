Weekly MySQL News

Unofficial Weekly News Digest of all things MySQL

Hello, MySQL Community

We started this newsletter because we wanted to provide a newsletter to the MySQL community of all things MySQL. We are technologists, hackers, and believers in open source, and we wanted to create a better, simpler summary of things going on in the community.

List Concept

We will send out weekly summaries of exactly 10 MySQL related articles. Nothing more, nothing less. We will handpick content and distribute it every Friday.

List Privacy

We respect your right to privacy and will never share your email with any other email list, person or company, even our own. This list is maintained independently and you will be able to unsubscribe at anytime.
